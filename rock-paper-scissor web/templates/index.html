Overview of the game

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic hand game that's easy to learn and fun to play! The game is played between two players, typically using their hands to form one of three shapes: rock, paper, or scissors.Each shape has a rule about which other shape it defeats:

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
